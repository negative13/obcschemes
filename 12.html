Name of the scheme:

Grant in aid for running hostels for OBC students.

Objectives of Scheme:

To enable OBC organizations to run hostels for OBC students.

Eligibility Criteria:

Organisation wherein 75% students from OBC and 25% SC.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Rs. 750/- pm. for 10 months, rent Rs. 5000/- for urban, Rent Rs.2500/- for rural, Honororium Superitendent Rs.4000/- pm Clerk-cum-Attendant, Rs.2500/- pm, Cook Rs.1500/- pm

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application to be filled and submitted through concerned institution

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Caste certificate
2. Aadhar card
