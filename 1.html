Name of the scheme:

Prashikshan Yatra Notified vide No:61/31/2015/SC-OBC/SWD/7100 dt.4/2/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To provide financial assistance to the educational institutes in the OBC dominated areas to conduct study tours/ Excursions across the country to cover places of historic, cultural and educational importance during school vacations.

Eligibility Criteria:

Educational Institutes in Goa recognised by Goa Board/CBSE having OBC Community students can seek financial assistance to conduct tours/ excursions for OBC community student.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Financial assistance to the educational institutes for an amount up to Rs.1.00 lakh to conduct study tours/ excursions and exp. of actual travelling cost upto 40 students and 2 teachers.

Procedure to avail benifits:

Head of the educational institute shall submit application in prescribed form along with brief report on the purpose of excursion, expected outcome of the tour list of the students in the prescribed proforma to Social Welfare Department.

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Application in prescribed form along with brief report on the purpose of excursion, expected outcome of the tour list of students in the prescribed proforma.
2. Written permision of the educational and with confirm rail/bus tickets of the students/guide teacher.
3.Application should be submitted atleast one month in advance.
4.Aadhar card.
