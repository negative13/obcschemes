Name of the scheme:

Saral Vidya Sahay scheme (Stipend to OBC students) Notified vide No: 62-2-04-BC(136) dt 8/6/2006

Objectives of Scheme:

The objective of the scheme is to improve the educational status assist the tendancy of dropout OBC students by the way of providing financial assistance to the family of such students.

Eligibility Criteria:

1. The applicant should be a bonafide resident of the state of Goa.
2. The applicant should be regular student of an Institution.
3. The combined income of parents Rs. 1,20,000/- p.a.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

1. Std V to VIII Rs.225/- pm (for 11 months)
2. Std IX to X Rs.275/- pm (for 11 months)

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application to be filled and submitted through concerned institution.

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Income certificate
2. Caste certificate
3. Bank pass book copy
4. Marksheet
5. Aadhar card.
