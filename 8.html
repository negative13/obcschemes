Name of the scheme:

Gagan Bharari Shiksha Yojana Notified vide No: 61/32/2015/SC-OBC/SWD/7097 dt. 4/2/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To provide additional finance assistance to the OBC students as the maintenance allowance given under the post matric scholarship is inadequate to meet the expenses on food and travel.

Eligibility Criteria:

OBC students must have availed for eligible for centrally sponsored post matric scholarship during the current academic year.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Additional maintenance allowances of Rs. 750/- p.m. for day scholare and Rs. 1500/- p.m. for those staying in hostel during the academic year allowance of Rs.750/- academic year (for a period of ten p.m. During the months is given.)

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application in prescribed format to be submitted to Directorate of Social Welfare.

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Caste certificate
2. Income certificate
3. Attested copies of mark sheet.
4. Attested copy of fees paid
5. Hostel certificate (if applicable)
6. Copy of Aadhar Card
7. Bank account
8 Pre-receipt
