Name of the scheme:

Sanskriti Bhavan Notified vide No. 61/33/2015/SC-OBC/SWD/7098 dt. 4/2/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To help the OBC community by providing needed infrastructure of Multipurpose community halls in the villages dominated inhabited by OBC community.

Eligibility Criteria:

Sanskriti Bhavan will be used as a multipurpose community hall by the local population. It will have facility for special Anganwadi, reading room, recreation room. Hall with a seating capacity of 200 to 300 people.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Total cost of the project including the cost of land shall not exceed Rs. 50.00 lakhs.

Procedure to avail benifits:

Application in prescribed form may be made by village Panchayat or group os OBC or NGO working for the betterment of OBC in V.P area

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Details of the land including the copies of ownership of document.
2. Resolution of the V.P.
3. Incase the request is made by a group of OBC signatures of atleast 100 persons belonging to OBC community.
4. NOC from the owner of the land
5. Survey records and survey plan of the propety.
