Name of the scheme:

Antya Sanskar Sahay Yojana Notified vide No: 61-30-2015/SC-OBC/ASSY/SWD/7091 dt. 4/2/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To grant financial assistance for conducting funerals and religious ceremonies related to last rites of the deceased OBC families to have funeral in decent manner.

Eligibility Criteria:

Any OBC family whose income is less than Rs. 2.00 lakhs per annum can apply.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Maximum financial assistance under this scheme for Funeral is Rs. 20,000/-

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application in prescribed form to be submitted to Directorate of Social Welfare

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Caste certificate issued by competent authority.
2. Income certificate issued by the Mamlatdar.
3. Death certificate of the Deceased. 4. Receipt of bills/ Proof of exp.
5. Aadhar card.
