Name of the scheme:

Support to Orphan Child/Children of a widow, Notified Vide no. 61-36-2015/SC-OBC/SWD/4102 dated . 04/02/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To give financial support to the orphan children of widows for meeting the expenditure on food, clothing, and shelter till the children attains the age of 18 years.

Eligibility Criteria:

1. Any widow who is a DSSS beneficiary belonging to OBC community and having minor children is eligible, subject to the benefit being limited to 2 children.
2. Any orphan who is under the care of a gaurdian,viz. grandparents or near relatives or in a protective home/ Dharmasala etc upto the age of 18 years.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

A. Fixed maintenance allowance of Rs 1500/- per month, per child of a widow.
B. Fixed maintenance allowance of Rs. 2000/- per month, per child who is an orphan.

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application in prescribed format to be submitted to the Directorate of Social Welfare.

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Marriage certificate of the applicant.
2. Death certificate of the husband of the applicant.
3. OBC community certificate issued by the competent authority.
4. Income certificate.
5. DSSS beneficiary evidence.
6. Aadhar card.
