Name of the scheme:

Mundkarache Ghar Notified vide No. 61/29/2015/SC-OBC/SWD/7101 dt. 4/2/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To provide financial assistance to the needy mundkars of OBC to enable them to excercise their right to purchase dwelling house.

Eligibility Criteria:

Any OBC family whose income from all sources should not exceed Rs.3.0 lakhs p.a. person should declared or registered as mundkar of the dwelling house. Order to determine purchase price is passed by the Mamlatdar under the provisions of the Goa Daman and Diu Mundkar Act.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Maximum financial assistance under beneficiary from his own sources Mundkar Act to be borne by Land area ceiling of 200 square meters in urban and 300 square meters in rural area. Land price for the area exceeding the land ceiling prescribed by the scheme for purchse of dwelling house is Rs. 30,000/-

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application in the prescribed form to be submitted to the Directorate of Social Welfare.

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Certified copy of Election.
2. Certified copy of Ration card
3. Form I & XIV
4. Residence certificate
5. Caste certificate/ income certificate
6. Proof tenant residing since birth
7. Undertaking from the co-owner that they will not sell the said house or land
8. Consent between Bhatkar and Mundkar regarding the selling and purchase of the land.
9. NOC for purchase of land.
