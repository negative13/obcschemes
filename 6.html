Name of the scheme:

Post matric scholarship for OBC students Notified vide dt. 1/7/2011

Objectives of Scheme:

The objective of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to the OBC students studying at Post matriculation or post-secondary stage to enable them to complete their education.

Eligibility Criteria:

1. To provide financial assistance to students belonging to OBC whose income does not exceed Rs. 1.00 lakhs p.a.
2. Scholarship will be given for the study of all recognised post-matriculation or post-secondary courses pursued in recognised institutions.

Pattern of assistance:

Central Sector Scheme(100% over and above committed liability)

Assistance available under the scheme:

GROUP A
Medical/ Engineering including Degree level courses in Indian Medicine etc,
For Hostellers Rs. 750/- pm
For Day scholars Rs. 350/- pm
GROUP B
Diploma level courses in Indian Medicine, Engineering, Architecture, Printing technology, etc.,
For Hostellers Rs. 510/- pm
For Day scholars Rs. 325/- pm
GROUP C
Certificate coursing Engineering/ Technology/ Architecture and Medicine, Degree/ Post Graduate etc.,
For Hostellers Rs. 400/- pm
For Day scholars Rs. 210/- pm
GROUP D
General courses upto Graduate level (2nd year onwards) XIth and XIIth, Intermediate courses and 1st year of general courses upto graduate level
For Hostellers Rs. 260/- pm
For Day scholars Rs. 160/- pm


Procedure to avail benifits:

The application to be filled through concerned institution.

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Caste certificate
2. Income certificate
3. Marksheet, Fee receipt
4. Bank passbook copy
5. Aadhar card
