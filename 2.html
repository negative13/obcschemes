Name of the scheme:

Atal Asra Yojana for OBC Notified vide No. 61/34/2015/SC-OBC/SWD/7096 dt. 4/2/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To provide supplementary financial assistance for construction and repair and reconstruction of house to OBC.

Eligibility Criteria:

Any OBC family whose income is less than Rs. 1.50 lakhs per annum Families who have been sanctioned benefit/ eligible under the scheme of Rajiv Indira Awas Yojana or for the balance additional financial subject to the limit of maximum financial assistance prescribed under this scheme.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Maximum financial assistance under this scheme for construction of New house is upto 2.5 lakhs and for repair of existing house is up to Rs. 1,00,000/- including assistance under IAY or RAY. financial.

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application in prescribed form to be submitted to Directorate of Social Welfare.

Check-list of Documents required:

For construction of new house
1. Affidavit
2. Sanction order under IAY or RAY if any
3. Detailed estimate of Chartered Engineer/ Architect
4. Form I & XIV of the land in which house is to be constructed
5. ownership document of the land
6. Irrevocable NOC of the land owner
7. Mundkar order issued by the Mamlatdar or Panchayat re-construction license.
8. Any other evidence for its acceptance by the committee.
9. Income certificate
10. Caste certificate
11. Aadhar card or EPIC
12. copy of Ration card
13. Bank passbook copy

For repair of existing house. Besides all the above documents.
1. Panchayat repair licence or house tax receipt or any other evidence for acceptance.
