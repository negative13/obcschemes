Name of the scheme:

Meritorious scholarship to OBC students Notified vide No: 62-2-04-BC(136) dt 8/6/2006

Objectives of Scheme:

To encourage students who
1. Min 50% in Std V to VII
2. Min 60% in Std VIII to X

Eligibility Criteria:

1. The applicant should be a bonifide resident of the state of Goa
2. The applicant should be regular student of an Institution.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

Std V & VI Rs. 800/- p.a.
Std VII & VIII Rs. 900/- p.a.
Std IX Rs. 1000/- p.a.
Std X Rs. 1500/- p.a.

Procedure to avail benifits:

The application to be filled and submitted through concerned Institution

Check-list of Documents required:

1. Caste certificate
2. Marksheet
3. Aadhar card
