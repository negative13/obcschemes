Name of the scheme:

Merit based award notified vide No: 61/35/2015/SC-OBC/SWD/7099 dt. 4/2/2016

Objectives of Scheme:

To motivate students by giving merit based award based on the benchmark of the performance and to provide financial incentive to high performing students who are economically weak.

Eligibility Criteria:

Students will be eligible based on performance at the Board exams for an award of highest amount and whose annual family income is less than Rs. 2.00 lakhs.

Pattern of assistance:

State Sector Scheme

Assistance available under the scheme:

a. Students securing marks in the range (between 50% to 59.99%) will be given a financial award of Rs. 5000/-
b. Students securing marks in the range (between 60% to 69.99%) will be given a financial award of Rs. 8000/-
c. Students securing marks in the range (between 70% to 74.99%) will be given a financial award of Rs. 10,000/-
d. Students securing 75% marks and above will be given a financial award of Rs. 15000/-

Procedure to avail benifits:

Application to be made in the prescribed format of merit based award for OBC students along with the attested copy of marksheet to Social Welfare Department.

Check-list of Documents required:

1. One copy of passport size photograph with signatures of the student thereon.
2. An attested copy of Social Status certificate issued by Directorate of Social Welfare.
3. One attested copy of certificates of educational qualifications.
4. An income certificate issued by competent authority showing annual income of the parents/ gaurdians from all sources. Employed parents/ gaurdians may submit income certificate from their employer.
5. Birth certficate/ proof as Goan Origin.
6. A copy of bank passbook.
7. Resident certificate.
8. Aadhar card.
